--- layout: post title: "DNF 0.6.1 Released" date: 2014-08-28 15:02:39 +0100 author: Jan Šilhan visible: 1 categories: dnf --- In new release is fully supported history redo command and is completed implementation of repository-packages commands. DNF 0.6.1 introduces new config options (gpgkey, repo_gpgcheck and gpgcheck) and dnf-yum package. Aside from that a lot of bugs have been fixed. For more details take a look at release notes.
