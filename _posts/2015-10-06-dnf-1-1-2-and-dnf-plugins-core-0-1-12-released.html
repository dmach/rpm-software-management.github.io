--- layout: post title: "DNF 1.1.2 and DNF-PLUGINS-CORE 0.1.12 Released" date: 2015-10-06 09:52:26 +0100 author: Jan Å ilhan visible: 1 categories: dnf --- The new release of DNF and DNF-PLUGINS-CORE is coming to Fedora stable repositories. The `--downloadonly` option supported in yum is now available in DNF and repoquery from DNF-PLUGINS-CORE has extended it's functionality of reverse RPM tag queries (`--what*`) for glob patterns. Aside from that nearly 20 bug fixes have been made in this DNF stack release. For further details look at DNF and DNF plugins release notes.
